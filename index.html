Golf Point Emaar South Payment Plan

The payment plan for Golf Point at Emaar South has been thoughtfully designed to provide flexibility and convenience for potential buyers. Whether you are an investor or looking to purchase a home for yourself, the payment structure ensures that acquiring your dream property is as seamless as possible.

Upon booking your unit, a down payment of 10% of the property price is required to secure your apartment. This initial payment not only confirms your reservation but also sets the stage for the subsequent, well-distributed installments. The remaining balance is spread across the construction period, with milestone payments tied to the development progress. This approach allows you to manage your finances effectively while monitoring the construction of your future home.

One of the key highlights of the payment plan is the post-handover payment option. Upon completion of the project, which is anticipated to be within a few years, you will only need to pay a percentage of the total cost. The remaining balance can be settled over an extended period after you have taken possession of your property. This post-handover payment plan eases the financial burden and provides you with ample time to settle the total cost of your investment.

This buyer-friendly payment plan reflects Emaar’s commitment to making property ownership accessible and hassle-free. With a focus on flexibility, the payment structure for Golf Point at Emaar South is tailored to meet the needs of different buyers, ensuring that the process of acquiring a luxurious apartment is as smooth and straightforward as possible.
